Barbecue Roast Chicken

Preparation Time: 30 MIN

Servings: 4

Description: A delicious chicken dish marinated in barbecue sauce and roasted to perfection. Perfect for a family dinner or a weekend barbecue party.

Dietary Information

Dietary Information Non-Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in chicken thighs
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Marinate the chicken

In a bowl, combine barbecue sauce, minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

Place the chicken thighs in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.

2. Preheat the grill or oven

If using a grill, preheat it to medium-high heat. If using an oven, preheat it to 375°F (190°C).

3. Grill or roast the chicken

Remove the chicken from the marinade and shake off the excess. Grill or roast the chicken for about 25-30 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice barbecue glaze.

4. Serve

Remove the chicken from the grill or oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Serve hot and enjoy your barbecue roast chicken!
