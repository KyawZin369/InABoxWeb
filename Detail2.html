Bento Hotpot

Tasty Hotpot Option

Cooking Time: 2 hours

Description: Warm up your taste buds with this delightful Bento Hotpot recipe. It's a comforting and flavorful hotpot filled with a medley of ingredients that will make your taste buds dance. This hotpot is perfect for chilly evenings when you crave something soothing and delicious. The combination of chicken broth, fresh vegetables, tender chicken, and fragrant rice creates a hearty and satisfying meal that will keep you coming back for more. Whether you're a hotpot enthusiast or trying it for the first time, this Bento Hotpot is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Instructions

1. Prepare the hotpot

In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a simmer over medium heat. This aromatic broth forms the flavorful base of your Bento Hotpot.

2. Add the vegetables

Add the sliced mushrooms, carrots, celery, onions, and bell peppers to the simmering broth. Allow them to cook until they become tender and infuse the broth with their delicious flavors, which usually takes about 10-15 minutes.

3. Incorporate the chicken and rice

Stir in the cooked chicken and rice. This step brings heartiness and texture to your hotpot. Continue to simmer for another 10 minutes to ensure that all ingredients meld together perfectly.

4. Season and serve

Season your Bento Hotpot with soy sauce and a touch of sesame oil. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Serve your steaming hot Bento Hotpot and relish the comforting flavors and aroma. Enjoy!
